about DiKa

DiKa is a multimedia designer who has always been inspired by the visual arts of photography and cinema. In 2021, he began studying cinematography at the Saint Petersburg State Institute of Film and Television, where he explored the theory of light and color, composition, and frame construction, while gaining extensive hands-on experience in photography.

Initially, he was drawn to nature, street photography, and architecture. Today, he strives to work across a variety of genres, including portrait and documentary photography. He is open to collaboration with creative and inspiring individuals, both in photography and video production.
